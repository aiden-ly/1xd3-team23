Bad Web Design

Let's go over an example of bad web design. The website we chose to showcase is our very own university's OscarPlus Job Board.

We are greeted with a website a website that, for some reason, has two different navigation bars for the exact same webpages, despite being in different design schemes too. This can be confusing for users as it creates redundancy and inconsistency in navigation.

Scrolling down a bit more on the dashboard, we see a row of links that all have images that have failed to load. Even though the website has a few faults, at least it's consistent in design, right?

Wrong. Going to the co-op section of the Engineering tab shows us a page with a completely different design and colour scheme. Rather than using McMaster's iconic maroon colours again, it uses purple, along with a different font. All of the divs seem to be round as well, unlike the blocky structure we saw earlier.
